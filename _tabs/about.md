Welcome to **Mine the Gap** - the technical blog of the product and CAT teams behind Microsoft Power Automate Process Mining. 

## Our Mission

We provide deep technical guidance and real-world examples to help process analysts, data engineers and process intelligence practitioners to build solutions using **Microsoft Power Automate Process Mining** and othe Microsoft technologies. 
Our content focuses on advanced scenarios, integration patterns, and best practices derived from working directly with enterprise customers.

## What We Cover

- **Advance Analytics**: Tips and best practice recommendations on using full power of Power Automate Process Mining feature set
- **Architecture & Design Patterns**: Building scalable, maintainable process intelligence solutions
- **Advanced Integration**: Connecting Power Automate Process Mining with enterprise landscape and other Microsoft technologies and products
- **Authentication & Security**: Implementing secure authentication flows and managing permissions
- **Performance Optimization**: Tips for improving response times and handling high-volume scenarios
- **Troubleshooting & Debugging**: Common issues and their solutions from real implementations
